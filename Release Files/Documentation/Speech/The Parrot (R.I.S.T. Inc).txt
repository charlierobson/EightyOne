==============================
The Parrot for the ZX80 / ZX81
R.I.S.T. Inc.
==============================

The Parrot uses port $FF4F to write to and read the status back from the SP0256.
Only address lines A7, A5 and A4 are decoded.

When reading from the Sp0256, the 'busy' status is reported in bit 0. The bit
has a value of 0.
